Your job posting will be visible for the members of the platform for 30 days. After that period it will dissapear from job hub. You can drop the posting earlier: go to Profile Settings > Job Feed. We recommend to drop job posting earlier if you found the right candidate or don't wish to hire anymore. If you will not do it, you might still recieve applications for a job from the other members. SAY THAT YOU WILL HAVE TWO BUTTONS TO LET KNOW THE PLATFROM PEOPLE THAT YOU WANT TO EITHER DROP POSTING OR THAT YOU HIRED SOMEONE. DONT LET THE PEOPLE TO DO ANY WORK ON THE PLATFROM!
