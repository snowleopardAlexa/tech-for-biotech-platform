How it works

The Emblem System is a principal form of communication on our platform. Each person had been assigned a badge that represents the industry or the genre it belongs to. For example, a violet badge with a letter C represents Company and it is assigned to all the users who registered themselves on the platform as a company.

The members of the platforms can search for specific users thanks to our Emblem Algorithm. We pair the users with their needs. If you have a startup we pair you with investors on our platform. If you are a scientist who wants to share your studies, we pair you with other scientists. The badge is assigned to you during the registration process but you get full control of what you need at a certain time. You can personalize your profile by changing the industry, adding or removing emblems from your profile. If you are an investor who does not want to invest anymore, you can drop the black badge and our algorithm will modify the search to your needs. You will no longer be highlighted as an investor. You will no longer receive unwanted messages pleading for funding.

The Emblem System works on the events that are created through our platfrom. Imagine that you attend the event that gathers 1,000 of people. Imagine now that you come to this event in order to find the necessary money for your startup, or to find Ph.D. internship, or to get hired by the company. You come with a goal and we want you to succeed in finding what you need. The event lasts 3 hours and you must act fast. You need to connect with the people you need. Chatting with random individuals will not lead you anywhere. Remember, you are on the mission. Each attendee of our events gets a personalized badge in a form of a sticker. A sticker is pegged to the shirt of the person what makes it visible from the distance. The colors attract people's attention. You come to our event and you see 1,000 of people but each person has an emblem on its chest. Colors will guide you to the right person. Take advantage of that opportunity.

How it works

The Need System consists of job, funding and co-founder emblems. You either enable or disable those emblems on your profile. If you look for a job, you leave the looking for a job mode on, if you do not - you switch the mode to off. The same rules apply to looking for funding or co-founder modes.

The Need Algorithm will show your profile to the companies that look for employees, to investors that want to invest in startups and to the individuals with similar interests who might become your partners. Thanks to The Need System you control your surroundings and speed the process of getting what you need.

How it works

The Emblem Award is an exclusive one and it is assigned only to the leaders of the platform. Tech for Biotech prizes those who organize the events through the platform. We believe that in-person meeting is the key to smooth communication and we encourage our users to create events. Even if you will create just one event that will take place, you will be assigned an award badge. The badge will be visible on your profile to all the users of the platform.
