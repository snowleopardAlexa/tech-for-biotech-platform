Looking for a job mode

When "Looking for a job" mode is on, you can see the clover under your profile photo. The clover is visible to all the members of the platform.

Please note that when the mode is off, the clover is not visible under your profile photo.

Do you want to turn on "Looking for a job mode?"

On Off

Please keep the mode on only when you are looking for a job. Otherwise, you might recieve unwanted invitations for job interviews.
