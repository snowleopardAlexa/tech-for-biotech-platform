Post Job

Your job posting will be visible for the members of the platform for 30 days, after that period it will dissapear from job hub. You can drop the posting earlier if you wish. Go to Profile Settings > Job Feed, in order to manage your job postings. Please note that we recommend to drop job posting earlier if you found the right candidate or don't wish to hire anymore since you might still recieve applications for a job from the other members.
